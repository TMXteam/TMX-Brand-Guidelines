02● Which TMX is this

Three products, one house. Each owns one accent inside its own surfaces. This kit is TMX Ecosystem; for Predixa or TMX DEX, use that product's kit.

04● Clear-space & size

Give it air.

Clear space on every side equals the height of the shield's short side. Nothing enters that zone: copy, another logo, a frame edge. The dashed box is the minimum; more is fine.

Hard ruleMinimum size.

20px tall on screen, 8mm in print. Below that, use the favicon build (favicon.svg, favicon-16/32.png) rather than scaling the mark down.

Hard ruleTerracotta is the only accent.

Terracotta is the only accent, on both fields. No neon, gold, cyan, jewel-tone or glow on an umbrella surface. No stacked colours, no rainbow data-viz. The foil gradient is for the single italic clause and the one display numeral only; never body copy or buttons.

Reproduce whichever field your placement sits in; campaigns lead in light (§08).

Type rules
  • Single italic per fold. Italic body copy is forbidden.
  • 11px floor for any readable label; never below the faint alpha (0.64 dark / 0.66 light).
  • Fraunces is Medium Italic at weight 500, optical size 14. No static Medium exists upstream, so both are cut from the variable font. Optical sizes 72 and 144 are finer display cuts for print and campaign work, in the zip below.

07● Co-brand lockups

Two marks, equal weight.

Build the lockup horizontally: two marks at equal optical weight, a thin neutral-gray divider between, clear space on both sides equal to the mark's clear-space unit. The lead brand decides the order.

Partner logo

Neither mark sits below its own minimum size (§04).

Hard ruleNever generate the lockup with AI.

Compose lockups by hand from the supplied vector marks. Don't recolour either logo, don't mix a stacked mark with a horizontal one, and don't let an image generator redraw the mark; it gets the proportions and clear-space wrong.

08● Ad laws · design slice

The mark leads.

Hard ruleIn an ad, the name never travels alone.

Lead with the mark + tmx.info. In an ad the name appears only when anchored to both, never alone. A bare name is what an impersonator runs; the mark plus the real domain is how a viewer tells the genuine article apart. Inside owned surfaces (your site, docs, this kit) the name is used normally.

The rest of the ad spec

  • Lead in light. Campaigns default to warm paper, terracotta leading on both fields, one accent per frame; reach for the dark frame only when the placement itself is dark.
  • The CTA is a terracotta tmx.info pill. The real domain, spelled out.
  • Don't invent figures and don't name a competitor. Any number must be true, current, and sourced from the live product; if it can't be sourced, cut it.

Naming & manifest

The shield is logo/ecosystem-logo[-variant] and the emblem is globe/ecosystem-globe-{dark,light}; -mono is the one-colour vector, -white / -black are the reversed and silhouette rasters, and numeric suffixes are pixel sizes. A manifest.md at the pack root lists every file and its use.
