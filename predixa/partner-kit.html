Reproduce Predixa exactly as it is.

This kit exists for one job: rendering Predixa correctly in a campaign. It is the design spec, not a rulebook on permission. Every value here is drawn from the same source the product is built from.

The one hard rule

In a paid placement, lead with the shield and predixa.xyz. The name only appears anchored to them, never alone.

● 01 · How to use this kit

Reproduction, not approval.

You are reproducing Predixa, TMX's wallet-native prediction market, at predixa.xyz: the mark, the colour, the type, the co-brand lockup. Sections marked Hard rule are non-negotiable; everything else is the spec to match.

Whether you may run a placement, and the full usage terms, live in the usage & trademark policy. Questions, or a layout to sign off? team@tmx.info.

02● Which TMX is this

Three brands, one house.

TMX is one house with three products, and each product owns one accent inside its own surfaces. This kit renders Predixa; if your campaign is about the TMX umbrella or TMX DEX, stop here and use that product's kit.

TMX Ecosystem tmx.info Terracotta, both themes
Predixa You are here predixa.xyz Gold #FFB627 by night, emerald #0E7A40 by day
TMX DEX tribe-dex.com Cyan + jewel tones
Hard ruleAccents stay in their lane.

Predixa is gold (dark) / emerald (light). Never dress it in the umbrella's terracotta or DEX's cyan and jewel tones, and never put Predixa's gold on an umbrella surface. Reaching for a neon glow on Predixa is DEX's move, not ours.

On first mention, always pair "TMX" with a product name or the tmx.info domain.

03● Logo & wordmark

The two-sided mark.

Predixa's logomark is a shield with two facets (a gold facet and a green facet), the same duality that runs through the product: yes / no, gold by night / emerald by day. It sits on both themes unchanged; the field behind it changes, the mark does not.

Resting on the shield is a four-leaf clover, green with a gold edge, so it belongs to both facets at once. It is part of the core mark, not an ornament laid over it, and it ships inside every export below. Reproduce it as-is: never redraw it, rescale it against the shield, or swap in a stock clover.

04● Clear-space & size

Give it air.

Keep clear space around the mark equal to the height of the shield's short side on every side. Nothing (copy, another logo, a frame edge) enters that zone. The dashed box below is the minimum; more is fine.

Hard ruleMinimum size.

20px tall on screen, 8mm in print. Below that the shield's facets stop reading clearly; use the provided favicon build (favicon.svg, favicon-16/32.png) rather than scaling the full mark down yourself.

05● Colour

One accent, two daylights.

Predixa owns a single chromatic accent, but it is a different hue per theme by design. Dark is canonical gold; light is emerald, the green facet of the mark, never a tinted gold. This is a deliberate decision, not a mechanical inversion.

Hard ruleGold never touches paper.

On the light field the accent is emerald only; gold #FFB627 never appears there. One accent per surface: no stacked colours, no rainbow data-viz. The foil gradient is reserved for the single italic clause and the one display numeral; never on body copy or buttons.

Two themes, one identity: gold leads the dark field, emerald the light (campaigns lead in light, §08), never a mechanical inversion of the other. And never pure #000 or #FFF.

Type rules
  • Single italic per fold. The Fraunces foil clause appears once per screen. Italic body copy is forbidden.
  • 11px is the floor for any readable label. Never set low-alpha micro-type below the faint floor (0.64 dark / 0.66 light).
  • Load the three families from Google Fonts, or embed them. Don't substitute a lookalike.
07● Co-brand lockups

Two marks, equal weight.

When Predixa appears beside your logo, build the lockup horizontally: the two marks at equal optical weight, separated by a thin neutral-gray divider, with clear space on both sides equal to the shield's own clear-space unit. The lead brand decides the order.

Partner logo

Horizontal lockup: partner mark · thin neutral divider · Predixa shield. Neither mark sits below its own minimum size. When the partner leads, Predixa takes the second position at matched optical weight (the "Predixa secondary" variant) but never shrinks below the §04 floor.

Hard ruleNever generate the lockup with AI.

Co-brand lockups are composed by hand from the supplied vector marks. Don't recolour either logo, don't mix a stacked mark with a horizontal one, and don't let an image generator redraw the shield; it will get the facets, proportions, clear-space, and the clover wrong.

08● Ad laws · design slice

Lead with the mark.

Display advertising has one guiding rule, and it is a scam-defence as much as a style choice.

Hard ruleIn an ad, the name never travels alone.

Lead with the shield and the domain predixa.xyz. You may use the name "Predixa" in an ad, but only anchored to the mark and the real domain, never floating on its own. A bare name is exactly what an impersonator runs; the mark plus predixa.xyz is how a viewer tells the genuine article apart. Inside owned surfaces (your site, docs, this kit) the name is used normally.

The rest of the ad spec

  • Lead in light. Campaigns default to warm paper, gold leading on dark and emerald on light, one accent per frame; reach for the dark frame only when the placement itself is dark.
  • The CTA is an accent predixa.xyz pill (gold on ink, emerald on paper). Reinforce the real domain: buy only at predixa.xyz.
  • Don't invent figures and don't name a competitor. Any number must be true, current, and sourced from the live product; if it can't be sourced, cut it.

Files are grouped into logo/, globe/, favicon/, and fonts/.
