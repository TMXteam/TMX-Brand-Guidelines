Reproduce Predixa exactly as it is.

This kit exists for one job: rendering Predixa correctly in a campaign. It is the design spec, not a rulebook on permission. Every value here is drawn from the same source the product is built from.

The one hard rule

In a paid placement, lead with the shield and predixa.xyz. The name only appears anchored to them, never alone.

● 01 · How to use this kit

Reproduction, not approval.

You are reproducing Predixa, TMX's wallet-native prediction market, at predixa.xyz. This kit tells you how to render the brand so it reads as ours: the mark, the colour, the type, the co-brand lockup, and the hard rules that protect it in a paid placement.

What this kit is (and isn't)

  • It's a reproduction spec. How to place the mark, which accent per theme, what a co-brand lockup looks like, what a display ad may and may not do.
  • It isn't a permission form. Whether you may run a placement, and any sign-off, is agreed with your TMX contact, not decided here.
  • Every value traces to source. Hex, type, and spacing come from Predixa's own design tokens; don't substitute values from memory or a screenshot.

How to read it

Sections marked Hard rule are non-negotiable; breaking one makes the work off-brand or, in an ad, a scam risk. Everything else is the spec to match. If your campaign is actually about TMX DEX or the TMX umbrella, stop here and use that product's kit; §02 shows how to tell the three apart.

Questions, or want a layout signed off before it ships? Email team@tmx.info.

02● Which TMX is this

Three brands, one house.

TMX is one ecosystem with a deliberate volume dial. The umbrella is quietest; Predixa sits in the middle; TMX DEX is loudest. Each owns its accent inside its own routes and nowhere else. Reproduce Predixa at its own volume; don't borrow another product's colour to make it louder or quieter.

04● Clear-space & size

Give it air.

Keep clear space around the mark equal to the height of the shield's short side on every side. Nothing (copy, another logo, a frame edge) enters that zone. The dashed box below is the minimum; more is fine.

Hard ruleMinimum size.

20px tall on screen, 8mm in print. Below that the shield's facets stop reading clearly; use the provided favicon build (favicon.svg, favicon-16/32.png) rather than scaling the full mark down yourself.

Type rules
  • Single italic per fold. The Fraunces foil clause appears once per screen. Italic body copy is forbidden.
  • 11px is the floor for any readable label. Never set low-alpha micro-type below the faint floor (0.64 dark / 0.66 light).
  • Load the three families from Google Fonts, or embed them. Don't substitute a lookalike.
07● Co-brand lockups

Two marks, equal weight.

When Predixa appears beside your logo, build the lockup horizontally: the two marks at equal optical weight, separated by a thin neutral-gray divider, with clear space on both sides equal to the shield's own clear-space unit. The lead brand decides the order.

Partner logo

Horizontal lockup: partner mark · thin neutral divider · Predixa shield. Neither mark sits below its own minimum size. When the partner leads, Predixa takes the second position at matched optical weight (the "Predixa secondary" variant) but never shrinks below the §04 floor.

Hard ruleNever generate the lockup with AI.

Co-brand lockups are composed by hand from the supplied vector marks. Don't recolour either logo, don't mix a stacked mark with a horizontal one, and don't let an image generator redraw the shield; it will get the facets, proportions, and clear-space wrong.

08● Ad laws · design slice

Lead with the mark.

Display advertising has one guiding rule, and it is a scam-defence as much as a style choice.

Hard ruleIn an ad, the name never travels alone.

Lead with the shield and the domain predixa.xyz. You may use the name "Predixa" in an ad, but only anchored to the mark and the real domain, never floating on its own. A bare name is exactly what an impersonator runs; the mark plus predixa.xyz is how a viewer tells the genuine article apart. Inside owned surfaces (your site, docs, this kit) the name is used normally.

The rest of the ad spec

  • Accent by theme. Gold leads on dark, emerald leads on light. One accent per frame.
  • Frost is fine; neon is not. Predixa may use frosted glass on data panels; it never glows. Save the neon for DEX.
  • The CTA is a gold predixa.xyz pill. Reinforce the real domain: buy only at predixa.xyz.
  • One Fraunces foil clause per frame, a tiny mono risk line, the shield as the calm anchor. No symmetric text-on-both-sides banners.
  • Don't invent figures and don't name a competitor. Any number must be true, current, and sourced from the live product; if it can't be sourced, cut it.
09● Light / dark laws

Two designed themes.

Dark is canonical; light is a designed daylight twin, not an inversion. Reproduce whichever theme your surface is in, and keep the devices that belong to each.

On paper, the status text pair measures: down #C0392B at 4.9:1 (AA), up #1F9E57 at 3.1:1 (large text only).

The invariant

Off-black on dark, off-white on paper, always. Never #000, never #FFF. Status fills are constant across themes; only the status text colour swaps to hold AA contrast on paper.

Files are grouped into logo/, globe/, favicon/, and fonts/.
