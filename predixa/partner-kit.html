●  01 — How to use this kit

One product. One block. Reproduction only.

This kit covers Predixa, one product in the TMX ecosystem. If your placement co-brands with TMX DEX or the TMX umbrella instead, use that product's kit, the accents and loudness are deliberately different (see §02).

What this is

A single self-contained file you can open, print, or hand to a designer. Every value here is read from the live source (globals.css), so what you reproduce matches what ships. Use the Print / PDF and Daylight / Night controls top-right to check both themes.

What this is not

  • Not an approval process. There is no sign-off gate here, just the reproduction rules.
  • Not the full brand book. Voice strategy, trademark, and governance live in the public guidelines on tmx.info.
  • Not a licence. Using these marks means following §07 (co-brand) and §08 (ad laws) as written.
●  02 — Which TMX is this

Three brands, one volume dial.

The TMX ecosystem runs one editorial umbrella and two independently-expressed products. They share a type system and off-black / off-white base, and differ by a deliberate loudness. Predixa sits in the middle. Reproduce it at mid volume, never as loud as DEX, never as quiet as the umbrella.

●  03 — Logo & wordmark

The two-sided shield.

●  04 — Clear-space & minimum size

Let it breathe.

Keep clear space around the mark equal to the height of the shield's short side on all four sides. Nothing, no copy, no other logo, no frame edge, enters that zone.

Minimum size: 24px tall on screen, 8mm in print for the shield. Below 24px, switch to the icon facet, never shrink the full mark past legibility.

Do

  • Give it clear space on every side, even in tight lockups.
  • Scale uniformly from the supplied vector, keep it crisp.
  • Drop to the icon facet below the 24px floor.

Don't

  • Crowd it with the partner logo or a headline.
  • Stretch, rotate, or reproportion the shield.
  • Shrink the full mark below its legibility floor.

Colour scoping
  • One accent per surface. Gold or emerald is the only chromatic emphasis, no stacked colours, no rainbow data-viz.
  • Gold never touches paper. On the light field the accent is emerald, never a tinted gold.
  • Never reach for a sibling's colour. DEX cyan and umbrella terracotta are off-brand on a Predixa surface.

●  07 — Co-brand lockups

Two marks, equal weight.

When your logo sits beside Predixa's, the lead brand decides order and both marks carry equal optical weight, separated by a thin neutral divider. Clear space between them is the shield's own clear-space unit, on both sides.

PARTNER
Equal optical weight Thin neutral divider Clear-space unit both sides Never below the 24px floor

Do

  • Match optical weight, size to the shield's cap height, not its bounding box.
  • Keep the divider a thin neutral gray, not an accent colour.
  • Use the "Predixa secondary" order when your brand leads the placement.

Don't

  • Mix a stacked lockup with a horizontal one across a campaign.
  • Recolour, tint, or add effects to either mark.
  • Generate the lockup with AI, compose it from the supplied files.
Hard rule

Never generate a co-brand lockup with an AI image tool. AI reconstructions distort the shield geometry and colour. Compose lockups only from the canonical files in §11.

●  08 — Ad laws (design slice)

Never write the name.

In a paid placement the word "Predixa" is not written out. The shield and the domain (predixa.xyz) carry the name. This keeps every placement recognisable by mark, not by typeset word, and consistent across the ecosystem.

✗ Don't — name typeset in the ad

✓ Do — mark + domain carry it
Predict with conviction.

Reproduction prohibitions
  • No typeset "Predixa" in paid creative, the mark and domain stand in for it.
  • Gold leads dark, emerald leads light. Never a gold accent on the paper field.
  • Frost is fine; neon and glow are not. Predixa is restrained, glow is DEX's signature, not Predixa's.
  • No invented figures. Every number must be true, current, and consistent across creatives.
●  09 — Light / dark reproduction laws

Both designed, neither inverted.

Dark is canonical; light is a designed daylight twin, not a colour inversion. Reproduce whichever theme the surface calls for, and follow the field and accent for that theme exactly.

Dark — canonical
Field #0A0A0C · ink #F2F2F2
Accent: gold #FFB627
Light — daylight twin
Field #F6F2E8 · ink #16140F
Accent: emerald #0E7A40
Theme laws
  • Off-black / off-white only. Never #000, never #FFF, in either theme.
  • No glow on paper. Frost and halos stay inside dark surfaces.
  • Status fills stay constant; only status text swaps to the AA pair (#1F9E57 up / #C0392B down) on paper.
●  10 — Do / Don't gallery

The quick reference.

Do

  • Reproduce the shield from the supplied vector, on ink or warm paper.
  • Use one accent per surface, gold on dark, emerald on light.
  • Let the domain (predixa.xyz) carry the name in ads.
  • Keep motion, if any, gated behind reduced-motion.
  • Reproduce Predixa at mid loudness, calm and specific.

Don't

  • Redraw, recolour, or AI-generate the mark or a lockup.
  • Put gold on the paper field, or a sibling's colour anywhere.
  • Typeset the word "Predixa" in paid creative.
  • Add neon, glow, or a rocket emoji, that reads as DEX or as hype.
  • Lock up beside "TMX Global," a separate, unrelated company.
